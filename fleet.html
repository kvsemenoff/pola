Our Fleet

Pola Maritime’s own fleet of Handy and Supramax sizes are all ice class, rightship, modern, and most are geared, with the rest of the ships chartered in ranging between Handy to Panamax sizes. Total of around 50 ships at any one time and increasing.

Handysize

15,000 - 40,000 dwt

Supramax

50,000 - 65,000 dwt

Panamax

70,000 - 90,000 dwt

Our Ice-class vessels can
navigate all over the world
any time of the year.

This variety in vessel sizes and class allows us to trade all year round, in most areas and in ports where restrictions and accessibility can be very difficult.

We transport all types of dry bulk commodities, clean and dirty cargoes, from ores, coal, grains, steels, agricultural products, bauxite, alumina, fertilizers and much more.
